About CMRIT Hyderabad

CMR Institute of Technology (CMRIT) is a private engineering college located in Hyderabad, India. It is part of the CMR Group of Institutions. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of engineering and technology. CMRIT is committed to providing quality education and fostering a culture of innovation and research.
